<P style="font-size: 10pt" align="justify"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02. Departure of
Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain
Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</B>

<P style="text-indent: 4%; font-size: 10pt" align="justify">On July&nbsp;28,
2011, the Board of Directors of lululemon athletica inc. (the
&#8220;Company&#8221;) increased the size of the Board of Directors from eight
to nine members and appointed Jacques Levy as a Class&nbsp;III director to
fill the newly-created vacancy. Mr.&nbsp;Levy will serve as a member of the
class of directors whose terms expire at the 2013 Annual Meeting of
Stockholders and until such time as his successor is duly elected and
qualified, or until his earlier resignation or removal. The Board of Directors
has determined that Mr.&nbsp;Levy is &#8220;independent&#8221; as contemplated
by The Nasdaq Stock Market and other governing laws and applicable regulations.
Mr.&nbsp;Levy will also serve as a member of the Audit Committee of
the Board of Directors.
Mr.&nbsp;Levy&#8217;s compensation for his service as a director will be
consistent with that of the Company&#8217;s other non-employee directors, as
described in the Company&#8217;s definitive proxy statement filed with the
Securities and Exchange Commission on April&nbsp;29, 2011, except that prior to
the Company&#8217;s 2012 Annual Meeting of Stockholders he will receive a pro
rata portion of the annual restricted stock and option grants made to
non-employee directors. Mr.&nbsp;Levy will also enter into the standard form
indemnification agreement for directors and certain officers (which was
previously filed as Exhibit&nbsp;10.16 to the Company&#8217;s Registration
Statement on Form&nbsp;S-1 dated July&nbsp;9, 2007, Registration
No.&nbsp;333-142477).

<P style="text-indent: 4%; font-size: 10pt" align="justify">There is no
arrangement or understanding between Mr.&nbsp;Levy and any other person
pursuant to which he was selected as a director. There are no relationships
between Mr.&nbsp;Levy and the Company or its subsidiaries that would require
disclosure pursuant to Item 404(a) of Regulation&nbsp;S-K.
