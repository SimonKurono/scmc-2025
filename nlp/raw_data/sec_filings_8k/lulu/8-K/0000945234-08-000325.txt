<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02.</B>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of
Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</B>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">(e)</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B><I>Christine M. Day Executive Employment Agreement</I></B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On July&nbsp;28, 2008, the Company entered into an Executive Employment Agreement with its Chief
Executive Officer, Christine M. Day (the &#147;<B><I>Employment Agreement</I></B>&#148;), defining the terms of Ms.&nbsp;Day&#146;s
employment with the Company following her appointment as Chief Executive Officer on July&nbsp;1, 2008.
The Employment Agreement supersedes and replaces in its entirety Ms.&nbsp;Day&#146;s prior offer letter,
dated December&nbsp;24, 2007, as amended January&nbsp;3, 2008.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Under the terms of the Employment Agreement, Ms.&nbsp;Day will receive an annual base salary of
CDN$550,000 beginning on July&nbsp;1, 2008. Ms.&nbsp;Day&#146;s target bonus under the Company&#146;s 2008 Executive
Bonus Plan will increase from 60% to 75% of her base salary, provided that specified Company and
individual performance goals are met. The Company will grant Ms.&nbsp;Day options to purchase 83,333
shares of common stock on August&nbsp;1, 2008, 83,334 shares of common stock on September&nbsp;2, 2008, and
83,334 shares of common stock on October&nbsp;1, 2008, each pursuant to the terms of the Company&#146;s 2007
Equity Incentive Plan. Ms.&nbsp;Day will also retain the right to receive options to purchase 41,667
shares of common stock on January&nbsp;7, 2009, 41,667 shares of common stock on January&nbsp;7, 2010, and
41,667 shares of common stock on January&nbsp;7, 2011. All such options will have an exercise price
equal to the fair market value of the common stock on the date of grant and will vest 25% per year
for four years on each anniversary of the effective grant date of the option. Ms.&nbsp;Day will receive
four weeks of paid vacation each year.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ms.&nbsp;Day agrees to serve as a director of the Company and its affiliates, and will not be
entitled to additional compensation for such positions. Upon the termination of the Employment
Agreement for any reason, Ms.&nbsp;Day agrees to resign from all such director positions. Ms.&nbsp;Day
further covenants that she will not serve as a director of more than two entities that are
unrelated to the Company, and agrees to obtain the advance consent of the Company&#146;s board of
directors prior to commencing any such service for an unrelated entity.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Company will reimburse Ms.&nbsp;Day for all reasonable out-of-pocket expenses and she is
entitled to participate in the employee benefit and fringe benefit arrangements generally available
to the Company&#146;s senior executive employees. Additionally, the Company will provide Ms.&nbsp;Day with
benefit coverage for her dependents up to a maximum amount of US$12,000, and will also reimburse
Ms.&nbsp;Day for the cost of supplemental term life insurance up to a
maximum amount of US$17,500 per
year.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Ms.&nbsp;Day&#146;s employment may be terminated by Ms.&nbsp;Day or by the Company at any time, with or
without cause. In the event Ms.&nbsp;Day voluntarily resigns or the Company terminates her employment
for cause, she will receive only her base salary then in effect and benefits earned and payable as
of the date of termination. In the event the Company terminates Ms.&nbsp;Day without cause, and subject
to her compliance with the surviving terms of the Employment Agreement and the Non-Compete,
Non-Solicitation and Non-Disparagement Agreement and execution of a full release, she will be
entitled to a minimum of 12&nbsp;months of base salary, which amount will be increased by two additional
months of base salary for each additional year of service that Ms.&nbsp;Day provides to the Company, up
to a maximum amount of 18&nbsp;months of base salary.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A copy of the Employment Agreement is attached as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1 to this Form 8-K and is
incorporated herein by reference.
</DIV>
