STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Vancouver, Canada &#150; April&nbsp;3, 2013 &#151; lululemon athletica inc. [NASDAQ: LULU; TSX: LLL] today provided an update on its black luon production issues. </FONT></P>

have met testing standards, it was on the low end of lululemon&#146;s tolerance scale and we have found that our testing protocols were incomplete for some of the variables in fabric characteristics. When combined with subtle style changes in
pattern, the resulting end product had an unacceptable level of sheerness. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">lululemon had taken steps prior to the black luon issue to bolster

what we believe are the contributing causes. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Work streams and actions include: </FONT></P>
