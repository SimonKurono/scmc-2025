<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;5.03 Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws.</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">On July&nbsp;1, 2011, lululemon athletica inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) filed a Certificate of Amendment to its Amended and
Restated Certificate of Incorporation (the &#147;Certificate of Amendment&#148;) with the State of Delaware, Division of
Corporations, in order to (i)&nbsp;effect a two-for-one forward stock split of the Company&#146;s Common Stock and Special Voting
Stock, (ii)&nbsp;increase the number of authorized shares of the Company&#146;s Common Stock from 200,000,000 to 400,000,000 and
to reduce the par value of the Common Stock from $0.01 to $0.005 per share, and (iii)&nbsp;increase the number of authorized
shares of the Company&#146;s Special Voting Stock from 30,000,000 to 60,000,000 and to reduce the par value of the Special
Voting Stock from $0.00001 to $0.000005 per share. A copy of the Certificate of Amendment is attached hereto as
Exhibit&nbsp;3.1 and is incorporated by reference herein.

<DIV align="right" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><b>Exhibit 3.1</b></DIV>
<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>CERTIFICATE OF AMENDMENT TO<BR>
AMENDED AND RESTATED<BR>
CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION OF<BR>
LULULEMON ATHLETICA INC.</B>



<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 3%">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;lululemon athletica inc. (the &#147;<U>Corporation</U>&#148;), a corporation organized and existing under and by
virtue of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (&#147;<U>DGCL</U>&#148;), does hereby certify as follows:


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 3%">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. The present name of the Corporation is lululemon athletica inc. The Corporation was originally
incorporated in the State of Delaware on November&nbsp;21, 2005 under the name of Lulu Holding Inc.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 3%">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2. ARTICLE IV, Section&nbsp;4.1, of the Corporation&#146;s Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation
presently reads as follows:


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">&#147;<U>Total Authorized Capital</U>. The total number of shares of capital stock which the Corporation shall have
authority to issue is Two Hundred Thirty Five Million (235,000,000) shares, consisting of: (a)&nbsp;Two Hundred Million
(200,000,000) shares of common stock, par value $0.01 per share (the &#147;<U>Common Stock</U>&#148;), (b)&nbsp;Thirty Million
(30,000,000) shares of special voting stock, par value $0.00001 per share (the &#147;<U>Special Voting Stock</U>&#148;), as
provided in Article&nbsp;VI, and (c)&nbsp;Five Million (5,000,000) shares of preferred stock, par value $0.01 per share (the
&#147;<U>Preferred Stock</U>&#148;). The Common Stock, Special Voting Stock and Preferred Stock shall have the rights,
preferences and limitations set forth below.&#148;


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt">and is hereby amended and restated in its entirety to read as follows:



<P align="justify" style="margin-left:4%; font-size: 10pt">&#147;<U>Total Authorized Capital</U>. The total number of shares of capital stock which the Corporation shall
have authority to issue is Four Hundred Sixty-Five Million (465,000,000) shares, consisting of: (a)&nbsp;Four
Hundred Million (400,000,000) shares of common stock, par value $0.005 per share (the &#147;<U>Common
Stock</U>&#148;), (b)&nbsp;Sixty Million (60,000,000) shares of special voting stock, par value $0.00005 per share (the
&#147;<U>Special Voting Stock</U>&#148;), as provided in Article&nbsp;VI, and (c)&nbsp;Five Million (5,000,000) shares of
preferred stock, par value $0.01 per share (the &#147;<U>Preferred Stock</U>&#148;). The Common Stock, Special Voting
Stock and Preferred Stock shall have the rights, preferences and limitations set forth below.



<P align="justify" style="margin-left:4%; font-size: 10pt">Effective upon the filing date of this Certificate of Amendment to Amended and Restated Certificate of
Incorporation, (i)&nbsp;every one (1)&nbsp;outstanding share of Common Stock shall be split into two (2)&nbsp;shares of
fully paid and non-assessable Common Stock and (ii)&nbsp;every one (1)&nbsp;outstanding share of Special Voting Stock
shall be split into two (2)&nbsp;shares of fully paid and non-assessable Special Voting Stock (the &#147;<U>Stock
Split</U>&#148;). The Stock Split shall occur without any further action on the part of the Corporation or the
holders of the Common Stock or Special Voting Stock and whether or not certificates representing such
holders&#146; shares prior to the Stock Split are surrendered for cancellation. The Corporation shall not be
obliged to issue certificates evidencing the shares of Common Stock or Special Voting Stock outstanding as a
result of the Stock Split unless and until the certificates evidencing the shares held by a holder prior to
the Stock Split are either delivered to the Corporation or its transfer agent, or the holder notifies the
Corporation or its transfer agent that such certificates have been lost, stolen or destroyed and executes an
agreement satisfactory to the Corporation to indemnify the Corporation from any loss incurred by it in
connection with such certificates.&#148;


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 3%">3.&nbsp;The foregoing amendment has been duly approved by the Board of Directors of the Corporation in accordance with
the provisions of Section&nbsp;141 and 242 of the DGCL.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 3%">4.&nbsp;The foregoing amendment has been duly approved by the written consent of the stockholders in accordance with
Sections&nbsp;228 and 242 of the DGCL and the Corporation&#146;s Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 3%">5.&nbsp;The foregoing amendment shall be effective on and as of the date of filing of this Certificate of Amendment
with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware.
