First Quarter Net Revenue Increases 69% to $138.3&nbsp;million<BR>
First Quarter Diluted EPS of $0.27</B>
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>Vancouver, Canada &#151; June&nbsp;10, 2010 </B>&#151; lululemon athletica inc. &#091;NASDAQ:LULU; TSX:LLL&#093; today
announced financial results for the first quarter ended May&nbsp;2, 2010.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">For the first quarter ended May&nbsp;2, 2010:
</DIV>


<DIV style="margin-top: 6pt">
<TABLE width="100%" border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="font-size: 10pt">

<TR valign="top" style="font-size: 10pt; color: #000000; background: transparent">

    <TD width="2%" nowrap align="left"><B>&#149;</B></TD>
    <TD width="1%">&nbsp;</TD>
    <TD>Net revenue for the quarter increased 69.3% to $138.3&nbsp;million from $81.7
million in the first quarter of fiscal 2009. Net revenue from corporate-owned
stores was $115.6&nbsp;million for the quarter, an increase of 58.5% from $72.9
million in the first quarter of fiscal 2009, and comparable-store sales increased
by 35% on a constant-dollar basis.</TD>
</TR>

<TR>
    <TD style="font-size: 6pt">&nbsp;</TD>
</TR><TR valign="top" style="font-size: 10pt; color: #000000; background: transparent">

    <TD width="1%">&nbsp;</TD>
    <TD>Gross profit for the quarter increased by 112.3% to $74.4&nbsp;million, and as a
percentage of net revenue gross profit increased to 53.8% for the quarter from
42.9% in the first quarter of fiscal 2009.</TD>
</TR>

<TR>
    <TD style="font-size: 6pt">&nbsp;</TD>
</TR><TR valign="top" style="font-size: 10pt; color: #000000; background: transparent">
    <TD width="3%" style="background: transparent">&nbsp;</TD>
    <TD width="2%" nowrap align="left"><B>&#149;</B></TD>
    <TD width="1%">&nbsp;</TD>

the first quarter of fiscal 2009.</TD>
</TR>

<TR>
    <TD style="font-size: 6pt">&nbsp;</TD>

million in the first quarter of fiscal 2009.</TD>
</TR>

    <TD style="font-size: 6pt">&nbsp;</TD>

</TR>
</TABLE>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">The Company ended the first quarter of fiscal 2010 with
$173.6 million in cash and cash equivalents compared to $59.3 million at the end of the first quarter of
fiscal 2009. Inventory at the end of the first quarter of fiscal 2010 totaled $50.8 million compared
to $44.6&nbsp;million at the end of the first quarter of fiscal 2009.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Christine Day, lululemon&#146;s CEO stated: &#147;This was our company&#146;s best-ever first quarter as we were
able to generate very strong same store sales growth in both the U.S. and Canada. In the first quarter, we saw our trailing 12
month average sales per square foot reach $1,428, an improvement from last quarter&#146;s $1,318.&#148;</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>Updated Outlook</B>
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">The previous outlook had reflected a tax rate of 35%. Consistent with the adjustment to the tax
rate in the first quarter of fiscal 2010 the updated outlook assumes a tax rate of 40% for fiscal
2010.</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">For the second quarter of fiscal 2010, we expect net revenue to be in the range of $140&nbsp;million to
$145&nbsp;million based on a comparable-store sales percentage increase in the mid 20s on a
constant-dollar basis. Diluted earnings per share are
expected to be in the range of $0.21 to $0.23 for the quarter. This assumes 72.1&nbsp;million diluted
weighted-average shares outstanding.</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">For the full fiscal 2010, we now expect net revenue to be in the range of $620&nbsp;million to $635
million and diluted earnings per share are expected to be in the range of $1.05 to $1.10 for the
full year. This assumes 72.2&nbsp;million diluted weighted-average shares outstanding.</DIV>
