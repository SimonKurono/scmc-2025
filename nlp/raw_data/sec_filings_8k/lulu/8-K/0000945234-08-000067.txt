<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02. Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of
Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</B>
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">On February&nbsp;18, 2008, Michael Tattersfield resigned his position as Executive Vice President,
Retail Logistics and Sourcing for lululemon athletica inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;), effective on the close
of business on April&nbsp;4, 2008 (the &#147;Separation Date&#148;). The Company&#146;s Chief Executive Officer,
Robert Meers, will assume the oversight responsibilities for the Company&#146;s sourcing, production and
logistics departments.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">In connection with his resignation, the Company has agreed to continue to pay Mr.&nbsp;Tattersfield&#146;s
base salary of CDN $392,111 for the 12-month period after the Separation Date. Mr.&nbsp;Tattersfield
will also be eligible to participate in the Company&#146;s tax equalization program in an amount not to
exceed US $35,000 and will be entitled to family medical benefits coverage for the 12-month period
after the Separation Date. In consideration of these payments and benefits, Mr.&nbsp;Tattersfield
entered into a release agreement pursuant to which Mr.&nbsp;Tattersfield provided the Company a general
release and agreed to certain restrictive covenants, including confidentiality, non-disparagement,
non-competition and non-solicitation provisions for a period of 12&nbsp;months after the Separation
Date.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">The summary of the material terms of the arrangements described above is qualified in its entirety
by reference to the Letter Agreement and Full and Final Release, a copy of each of which is
attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1 and incorporated herein by reference. The Company&#146;s press release
dated February&nbsp;19, 2008 announcing the resignation of Mr.&nbsp;Tattersfield as described above is filed
herewith as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>LULULEMON ATHLETICA ANNOUNCES RESIGNATION OF MICHAEL TATTERSFIELD</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>February&nbsp;19, 2008 &#151;  Vancouver, Canada </B>&#151; lululemon athletica inc. &#091;NASDAQ: LULU; TSX: LLL&#093;
today announced that Michael Tattersfield has resigned as Executive Vice President, Retail
Logistics and Sourcing due to personal and family reasons.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Mr.&nbsp;Tattersfield served as Chief Operating Officer of lululemon since joining the company in
October&nbsp;2006. In January&nbsp;2008, due to the rapid growth of the company, the role was split with Mr.
Tattersfield focusing on the leadership of the logistics and supply chain team. Christine Day, a
20&nbsp;year veteran of Starbucks, was hired to bring her extensive expertise in high growth retail
operations to lead the areas of Canadian, US and International store operations, wholesale and
phone sales, real estate development, and community relations.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Effective immediately, sourcing, production and logistics will report directly to Robert Meers, CEO
of lululemon athletica. Christine Day will continue to serve in her position as Executive Vice
President Retail Operations.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Mr.&nbsp;Meers, CEO of lululemon commented, &#147;Mike Tattersfield was instrumental in leading lululemon
through the impressive growth of 27 stores throughout 2007 and playing a key role in identifying
and hiring many of our senior management team. We are very appreciative of Mike&#146;s contributions
and wish him well in his future endeavors.&#148; Mr.&nbsp;Meers continued, &#147;We are fortunate to have a
strong team in place and fully operational. With the addition of Christine Day, who as head of
retail operations has already made significant contributions to the company, along with our skilled
executives in sourcing, logistics and production in place, I feel confident that there will be no
disruption to our business and that we will continue to seamlessly execute on our growth plans.&#148;
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt"><B>About lululemon athletica inc.</B><BR>
lululemon athletica (NASDAQ:LULU; TSX:LLL) is a yoga-inspired athletic apparel company that creates
components for people to live longer, healthier and more fun lives. By producing products that keep
people active and stress free, lululemon believes that the world will be a better place. Setting
the bar in technical fabrics and functional designs, lululemon works with yogis and athletes in
local communities for continuous research and product feedback. For more information, visit
www.lululemon.com <U>http://www.lululemon.com/</U>.
</DIV>
