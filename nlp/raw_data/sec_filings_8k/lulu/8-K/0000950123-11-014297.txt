<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events.</B>


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">On February&nbsp;15, 2011, we announced that, through our wholly-owned subsidiary, lululemon athletica canada inc., we
entered into a Purchase and Sale Agreement with 2725312 Canada Inc., effective as of December&nbsp;22, 2010, pursuant to
which we will purchase real property located at 1818 Cornwall Avenue in Vancouver, British Columbia, and a 130,000
square foot office building located on that site for an aggregate purchase price of $65,000,000 CAD. We intend to
continue using the property as our corporate headquarters. We currently expect to close the purchase of the property
on or around March&nbsp;1, 2011.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt">A copy of the press release we issued on February&nbsp;15, 2011 is attached to this current report as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1.

<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE</B>


<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Returning to Our Roots: lululemon athletica Comes Home to Kits</B>



<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt"><B><I>Vancouver, Canada, February&nbsp;15, 2011 </I></B><I>&#150; </I>lululemon athletica, inc. &#091;NASDAQ: LULU; TSX: LLL&#093;, the yoga-inspired athletic
apparel company, has come full circle and is settling in Kitsilano, the Vancouver community where it all started. It
was announced today that the company expects to complete the purchase of its Global Store Support Centre, located at
1818 Cornwall Avenue, on or around March&nbsp;1, 2011, subject to the satisfaction of certain closing conditions.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt">The building is 130,000 square feet; approximately 70,000 square feet have been occupied by more than 300 lululemon
athletica employees since late Fall 2010.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt">&#147;Kits has always been our home and we are thrilled that it once again will be,&#148; said Christine Day, lululemon athletica
CEO. &#147;To work in one of the most beautiful communities in Vancouver &#151; within walking distance to amazing yoga studios,
fitness facilities, the Seawall and beautiful beaches &#151; is what inspires us every day.&#148;


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt">1818 Cornwall &#151; lululemon&#146;s corporate headquarters &#151; is located at the base of the Burrard Street Bridge, overlooking
Kitsilano Beach and the North Shore mountains. The building originally operated as a bottling plant and was converted
into an office building in the 1980s.


<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt">&#147;Man, it feels good to be back! This is where it all started for us with our first store on West 4<SUP style="font-size: 85%; vertical-align: text-top">th</sup>,&#148; said
Chip Wilson, lululemon chairman and founder. &#147;This is where we belong and this is where we plan to stay.&#148;


<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt">-30-



<P align="justify" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>About lululemon athletica</B>
<BR>
lululemon athletica (NASDAQ:LULU; TSX:LLL) is a yoga-inspired athletic apparel company that creates components for
people to live longer, healthier and more fun lives. By producing products that help keep people active and stress
free, lululemon believes that the world will be a better place. Setting the bar in technical fabrics and functional
designs, lululemon works with yogis and athletes in local communities for continuous research and product feedback. For
more information, visit <U>www.lululemon.com</U>.
