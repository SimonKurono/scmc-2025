Third Quarter Diluted EPS of $0.20<BR>
Introduces Fourth Quarter Diluted EPS Outlook in the Range of $0.26 to $0.28</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>Vancouver, Canada &#151; December&nbsp;9, 2009 </B>&#151; lululemon athletica inc. &#091;NASDAQ: LULU; TSX: LLL&#093; today
announced financial results for the third quarter ended November&nbsp;1, 2009.

</DIV>

<DIV style="margin-top: 10pt">

    <TD width="1%">&nbsp;</TD>

quarter of fiscal 2008. Net revenue from corporate-owned stores was $98.1&nbsp;million, an
increase of 26.4% from $77.6&nbsp;million for the third quarter of fiscal 2008, and
comparable-store sales increased by 10% on a constant-dollar basis compared to the third quarter of fiscal 2008.</TD>
</TR>

</TABLE>

the third quarter of fiscal 2008.</TD>
</TR>

</TABLE>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>Updated Outlook</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><I>For the fourth quarter of fiscal 2009, we expect net revenue to be in the range of $140&nbsp;million to
$145&nbsp;million based on a comparable-store sales increase in the mid teens on a
constant-dollar basis. Diluted earnings per share are expected to be in the range of $0.26 to $0.28
for the quarter. This assumes a tax rate of 35% and 71.3&nbsp;million diluted weighted-average shares
outstanding.</I>
