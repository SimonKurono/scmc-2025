 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center">


<IMG SRC="g280022g09u24.jpg" ALT="LOGO">
 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2"><B>LULULEMON ATHLETICA INC. RAISES GUIDANCE FOR FOURTH QUARTER OF FISCAL 2011 </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2"><B>Vancouver, Canada &#150; January&nbsp;10, 2012</B> &#151; lululemon athletica inc. [NASDAQ: LULU; TSX: LLL] today announced that the Company is
raising its guidance for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2011 ending January&nbsp;29, 2012. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2">For the fourth quarter of fiscal 2011, the
Company now expects diluted earnings per share to be in the range of $0.47 to $0.49 as compared to its previous guidance range for diluted earnings per share of $0.40 to $0.42. The Company&#146;s improved guidance reflects stronger than anticipated
net revenue for the quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2">The Company now expects net revenue to be in the range of $358&nbsp;million to $363&nbsp;million for the
fourth quarter of fiscal 2011. This compares to the Company&#146;s previous guidance of net revenue in the range of $327&nbsp;million to $332&nbsp;million for the quarter, and compares to net revenue of $245 million for the fourth quarter of fiscal
2010. Comparable-store sales for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2011 are expected to reflect a low-to-mid twenties percentage increase on a constant-dollar basis. This compares to the Company&#146;s previous guidance of low to mid-teens
comparable-store sales for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2011. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2">Christine Day, lululemon&#146;s CEO stated: &#147;Our work throughout the
year building our inventory position is driving our success in the fourth quarter. Guests have responded exceptionally well to the robust assortment and bright color palette for holiday, and momentum continues with the new spring product
offerings.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2">Management will be presenting at the ICR XChange Conference on Wednesday, January&nbsp;11, 2012 in Miami, Florida. See
separate release for webcast information. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2"><B>About lululemon athletica inc. </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:10px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family:ARIAL" SIZE="2">lululemon athletica (NASDAQ:LULU; TSX:LLL) is a yoga-inspired athletic apparel company that creates components for people to live a long, healthy
