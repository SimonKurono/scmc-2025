<P style="font-size: 10pt" align="justify"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02. Departure of
Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain
Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</B>

<P style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 4%" align="justify">On
December&nbsp;10, 2008, the Board of Directors of lululemon athletica inc. (the
&#8220;Company&#8221;) increased the size of the Board of Directors from nine
to ten members and appointed Marti Morfitt as a Class&nbsp;II director to fill
the newly-created vacancy. Ms.&nbsp;Morfitt will serve as a member of the class
of directors whose terms expire at the 2009 Annual Meeting of Stockholders and
until such time as her successor is duly elected and qualified, or until her
earlier resignation or removal. The Board of Directors has determined that
Ms.&nbsp;Morfitt is &#8220;independent&#8221; as contemplated by The Nasdaq
Stock Market and other governing laws and applicable regulations.
Ms.&nbsp;Morfitt&#8217;s compensation for her service as a director will be
consistent with that of the Company&#8217;s other non-employee directors, as
described in the Company&#8217;s definitive proxy statement filed with the
Securities and Exchange Commission on May&nbsp;8, 2008, except that prior to
the Company&#8217;s 2009 Annual Meeting of Stockholders she will receive a pro
rata portion of the annual restricted stock and option grants made to
non-employee directors. Ms.&nbsp;Morfitt will also enter into the standard form
indemnification agreement for directors and certain officers (which was
previously filed as Exhibit&nbsp;10.16 to the Company&#8217;s Registration
Statement on Form&nbsp;S-1 dated July&nbsp;9, 2007, Registration
No.&nbsp;333-142477).

<P style="font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 4%" align="justify">There is no
arrangement or understanding between Ms.&nbsp;Morfitt and any other person
pursuant to which she was selected as a director. There are no relationships
between Ms.&nbsp;Morfitt and the Company or its subsidiaries that would require
disclosure pursuant to Item 404(a) of Regulation&nbsp;S-K.
